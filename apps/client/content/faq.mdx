## Security & Safety

**Q: Does Bike4Mind save my data?**  
A: Bike4Mind takes data privacy seriously. We do not store any types of data that you make; they are made by you and owned by you.

**Q: Is Bike4Mind being used anywhere?**  
A: Bike4Mind is designed for individual use and does not share user data publicly.

## Account

**Q: Are my personal information safe?**  
A: Yes, we prioritize the security of your personal information.

**Q: How do I turn on Dark/Light Mode?**  
A: You can toggle Dark/Light Mode in the upper left button that resembles a Moon or a Star (Depending on which mode you are in)
