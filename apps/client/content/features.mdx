# Features

## Profile - Your Personal Space

- Find all your content | Projects, Files, Notebooks, and more
- See what you're sharing
- Access items others have shared with you
- Manage your recent collaborations

## Sharing & Collaboration

- Keep it private
- Share with specific people
- Share with a group
- Share with everyone
Everything you can share has its own direct link for easy access

### Groups

Teams of users who can collaborate together. Each group has:

- Administrators and members
- Its own profile page
- Shared resources
- Custom AI settings (optional)
