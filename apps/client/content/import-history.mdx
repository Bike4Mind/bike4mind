# Importing Your Chat History

[Home](/) · [Guide](guide) · [FAQ](faq)

## Overview

Bike4Mind allows you to import your chat history from other AI platforms like OpenAI (ChatGPT) and Claude. This feature helps you consolidate all your AI conversations in one place.

## Supported Platforms

- ✅ **OpenAI (ChatGPT)** - Full conversation history import
- ✅ **Claude (Anthropic)** - Full conversation history import

## How to Export Your History

### From OpenAI/ChatGPT

1. Log in to [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com)
2. Click on your profile icon (bottom left)
3. Go to **Settings** → **Data controls**
4. Click **Export data**
5. OpenAI will email you a download link (usually within 24 hours)
6. Download the zip file from the email

### From Claude

1. Log in to [Claude.ai](https://claude.ai)
2. Click on your profile
3. Go to **Settings** → **Data & Privacy**
4. Click **Export data**
5. Anthropic will email you a download link
6. Download the zip file from the email

## Important: File Size Optimization

⚠️ **Large File Warning**: OpenAI exports with voice conversations can be **very large** (1+ GB) and may crash your browser during upload.

### The Problem

OpenAI exports include all audio recordings from voice conversations. These audio files:
- Can make the zip file over 1 GB
- Cause browser crashes during upload
- **Are not needed for import** (only text data is imported)

### The Solution

We provide a free optimization tool that reduces file size by **~95%** (1.1 GB → 51 MB) by removing unnecessary audio/video files.

## Using the Optimization Tool

### Option 1: Download the Script

1. Download the optimization script from our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/Bike4Mind/bike4mind/blob/main/scripts/optimize-openai-history.py)
2. Save it to your computer
3. Run the script:

```bash
python3 optimize-openai-history.py /path/to/your-export.zip
```

### Option 2: Manual Instructions

If you prefer to do it manually:

1. Unzip your OpenAI export
2. Keep only these files at the root level:
   - `conversations.json` ⭐ **(Required)**
   - `chat.html`
   - `message_feedback.json`
   - `shared_conversations.json`
   - `user.json`
3. Delete all conversation folders (folders with UUID names like `68b1167c-...`)
4. Delete all `file-*_data` folders
5. Re-zip the remaining files

**Note:** Only `conversations.json` is required for import. All other files are optional.

## Importing to Bike4Mind

### Step 1: Navigate to Settings

1. Click on your profile icon
2. Go to **Settings** or **Profile**
3. Look for **Import History** or **Upload LLM History**

### Step 2: Upload Your File

1. Click **Upload LLM History**
2. Select the source (OpenAI or Claude)
3. Choose your file:
   - **Recommended**: Use the optimized zip file (~50 MB)
   - **Not recommended**: Original large zip (may crash browser)
4. Click **Upload**

### Step 3: Wait for Processing

- Small files (< 100 MB): Usually complete in 1-5 minutes
- Large files (> 500 MB): May take 10-30 minutes
- You'll receive a notification when import is complete

## What Gets Imported?

### Conversations
- ✅ All conversation titles and timestamps
- ✅ Your prompts (messages you sent)
- ✅ AI responses
- ✅ Conversation history and context
- ✅ Created/updated dates

### Not Imported
- ❌ Audio/video files (not supported)
- ❌ Shared conversation settings
- ❌ Custom instructions or system prompts
- ❌ Temporary or deleted conversations

## Troubleshooting

### "Browser crashed during upload"
**Solution**: Your file is too large. Use the optimization tool to reduce file size.

### "Upload failed"
**Possible causes**:
1. File is corrupted - Try re-downloading from OpenAI/Claude
2. Invalid file format - Make sure it's a .zip file
3. Network timeout - Try uploading on a faster connection

### "Import completed but conversations are missing"
**Check**:
1. Was `conversations.json` in your zip file?
2. Did you export the correct date range?
3. Some very old conversations may not be included in exports

### "conversations.json not found"
**Solution**: Your zip file structure may be incorrect. Make sure `conversations.json` is at the root level of the zip, not in a subfolder.

## FAQ

**Q: Will this delete my conversations from OpenAI/Claude?**
A: No! Importing only creates a copy in Bike4Mind. Your original conversations remain unchanged.

**Q: Can I import multiple times?**
A: Yes, but duplicate conversations will be skipped or updated based on their IDs.

**Q: How long does import take?**
A: Typically 1-10 minutes depending on file size and number of conversations.

**Q: Is my data secure during import?**
A: Yes! Your data is encrypted during upload and stored securely. We never share or sell your data.

**Q: Can I export my Bike4Mind history?**
A: Yes! You can export your conversations at any time from your profile settings.

**Q: What about images in my conversations?**
A: Currently, only text content is imported. Image support is coming soon.

**Q: Can I delete imported conversations?**
A: Yes, you can delete individual conversations or all imported conversations from your session history.
