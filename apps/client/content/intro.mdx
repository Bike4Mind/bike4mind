## Key Values

- **Be Bold** - we are small and agile, we are obligated to be bold and iterate rapidly
- **AI Vendor Agnostic**, we integrate from all leading AI vendors
- **Just Works** - make the flows intuitive and make sense for the 80/20 cases, expose robust controls for the power users.

## Bike4Mind{'\u2122'} High-Level Design

***~Cognitive Workbench~*** - think like a well-stocked and comfortable woodshop - have all my bits of Knowledge, Files, Snippets, like jigs and artifacts

- **No vendor lock** - be AI vendor agnostic AND be AI vendor thirsty
- **Not just text based LLMs**, but integrate image generators like Scenario, voice like ELeven Labs, background removal, and more 'MOAR TOYS!'
- **Support the super set of features** from OpenAI, Athropic and Gemini - and be able to switch between them at will!
- Had to be web-based, and so react/next.js, MongoDB, AWS fabic serverd stateless via SST/SEED to be a modern architecture that is clean and efficient to operate
- And at the same time, do not pick any exotic new languages or stacks so as to make the funnel as wide as possible for ease of adoption
- **Destop dark mode** is the primary design but we also have **light mode**
- **Mobile** and **Progressive Web App** for you to use on your phone or tablet - or as a desktop app
- 5 levels of users: Demo, Paid, Premium, Manager and Admin, with re-skinning, with these 5 levels of users any business case should be able to be supported
- **Create a sharing platform**, where users may share their Notebooks, Files, Projects, share 1:1, 1:team, or 1:*
- **Create a marketplace** where these sharing efforts may optionally be monetized between the users
- **Implement all of our own algorithms** for Retrieval Augmented Generation, Prompt Marshalling, tool using and agent making, so as to avoid vendor lock
- And to make sure we understand how everything works and are able to pivoty quickly as the AI world changes hourly
