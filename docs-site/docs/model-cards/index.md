# Model Cards &amp; System Cards

This section preserves the **model cards** and **system cards** for the frontier
models that Bike4Mind builds on. We archive them here for two reasons:

1. **Durability.** Model access changes — models get deprecated, suspended, or
   pulled by vendors or regulators. The card outlives the model. Keeping a local
   copy means our reasoning, citations, and narrative work stay anchored to the
   document even when the live endpoint goes away.
2. **Grounding.** When we write about what a model can and cannot do — its
   safeguards, routing behavior, benchmark posture, alignment results — we want
   to cite the primary source, not our memory of a launch-day blog post.

## Available cards

- [Claude Fable 5 &amp; Claude Mythos 5](./fable-5-mythos-5.md) — Anthropic's
  Mythos-class frontier model, in its safeguarded (Fable) and less-constrained
  (Mythos) configurations. Access suspended June 12, 2026 under a US
  export-control directive; the system card remains published.
